Why Melbourne?

International Students

We offer a supportive environment for international students and are committed to ensuring our courses and research are relevant in a global context.

You will enjoy our location on the northern edge of Melbourne's vibrant city centre, which means a host of cafés, restaurants, shops, theatres, galleries and bars are just a short walk away.

Your connection to MGSE doesn’t end on graduation day. Our global alumni network is an important part of our work and a key to future career opportunities.
